## Is Rerolling Worth It?

Rerolling is **not mandatory**, but the source guide clearly recommends it if you can spare a little time. The published sheet estimates the modern reroll loop at roughly **7 to 8 minutes** because the skip function shortens the opening flow.

> If rerolling sounds miserable, skip it and start playing. It helps a fresh account, but it is not required to make progress.

- Character reroll helps your overall account progression.
- Arcana reroll matters more for the mid-to-late game than for the first few hours.
- The guide assumes you reroll on a **guest account** and only bind once you land the opener you want.

## Character Reroll Targets

The launch recommendation is straightforward: prioritize **Asherah Waltz**.

- **Primary goal:** secure **Asherah Waltz** at any usable star count, ideally with extra copies if the reroll is unusually strong.
- **Very strong extras:** Emilly, Bunnygirl Charlotte, Lacy, Frey Noble Princess, Bunnygirl Claire, and Bell Rhys.
- **PvP-only priorities:** Seira, Luna, and Elisa become more appealing if your main goal is rank play rather than stable PvE progression.

> The cleanest reroll rule is still: take **Asherah Waltz** first, then treat premium extras as bonus value instead of the main pass-fail check.

The sheet also notes that collaboration units like **Yoo Mina** and **Hilde** are not bad, but it still recommends prioritizing an **Asherah Waltz** account first.

## Arcana Reroll Targets

If you also want to chase Arcana on the same opening account, the source tab points to two main targets:

- **Best Arcana reroll goal:** **Lacy Arcana** or **Frey Noble Princess Arcana**
- **Usable even at low breakthrough:** both are considered worth keeping even at the base copy level

The guide also calls out a second tier of nice-to-have Arcana:

- Lyn
- Bunnygirl Claire
- Muriel
- Kyra
- Emilly
- Asherah Waltz

The practical advice from the sheet is to **focus on the character reroll first**, especially if chasing both character and Arcana is stretching the session too far.

## Farming and Substitute Arcana Notes

The source sheet repeatedly separates “must reroll” from “good but not mandatory.” That is the right lens to keep.

- **Charlotte Arcana** can be farmed through Fresh Point content at about **3 fragments per day**.
- **Asherah Waltz Arcana** can also be farmed, but the fragment drop rate is lower.
- **Petra Arcana** and **Naru Arcana** share the same core crit-rate skill, so Naru can substitute in some situations.

The source still adds one important free-to-play caveat: **Petra Arcana is extremely valuable for F2P accounts**, even if it is technically replaceable in a narrow skill-by-skill comparison.

## Quick Summary

If you want the shortest possible decision tree, follow this:

1. Reroll for **Asherah Waltz** first.
2. Treat extra premium companions as a bonus, not the primary check.
3. If you also hit **Lacy Arcana** or **Frey Noble Princess Arcana**, keep the account.
4. Do not overvalue farmable or replaceable Arcana during the first pass.
