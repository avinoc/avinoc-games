## Main Push Deck

This is the core deck Bullet used to progress through the main story stream:

- **Rosaria** — Primary DPS anchor
- **Waltz Asherah** — Secondary DPS / follow-up damage
- **Emily** — Flexible DPS that works across multiple attributes
- **Bunnygirl Charlotte** — Top-tier Sun DPS that carries most stages

This comp is "stacked with top-tier units" and performs well across most content. You can swap your DPS or tank attributes as needed to handle different boss matchups.

**Progression context:** Normal stages 30–35 (~340K recommended CP), all Hard mode cleared, Infinite Corridor Floor 292.

## Break Skill Picks

- **Cube Overload** — Primary break skill, used most frequently
- **Dimensional Assault** — Secondary option, also very solid

These two are the only break skills needed for most stage pushing.

## Sun Attribute Team

Sun units are used to break **Star-attribute** bosses.

| Unit | Role | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Bunnygirl Charlotte** | Main DPS | Extremely strong among Sun units. If you have her, always include her. |
| **Hilde** | Support / Break | Used alongside Charlotte for most stages. |
| Muriel | Optional | Sometimes used but not really necessary. |

This comp handled most Sun-vs-Star stages on its own.

## Moon Attribute Team

Moon is currently a very solid element with Emily and many strong DPS units.

| Unit | Role | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Emily** | Main DPS | Core Moon DPS, also used in the main push deck. |
| **Bunnygirl Frey** or **Bunnygirl Claire** | Secondary DPS | Either works well. Frey was also part of the high-score comp for the last Cosmo Gate. |
| Waltz Asherah | Situational | Sometimes not viable due to specific boss mechanics. When she can't be used, clear with a full Moon team instead. |

Moon teams were strong enough to clear bosses even when the main push deck's Waltz Asherah couldn't be used due to mechanics.

## Star Attribute Team

| Unit | Role | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Emily** or **Haydee** | Main DPS | Haydee is a very stable option. Cleared Floor 220 of Infinite Corridor. |
| **Bell** | Buff removal | **Currently the only unit that can remove buffs.** Essential for bosses that counter all incoming attacks — use Bell's ultimate to remove the counter buff. |
| **Dana** | Support | Surprisingly good with Rosaria. Dana gives Rosaria an ATK buff with her skill, very useful for manual play. |

Bell is particularly important for certain Infinite Corridor and Main Stream stages where bosses buff themselves to counter incoming damage.

## Order Attribute Team

| Unit | Role | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Waltz Asherah** | Main DPS | Follow-up attacks alone can break the boss — you don't need multiple Order units. |
| **Seira** or **Bunnygirl Charles** | Secondary | Either works fine. |
| Luna | Situational | Better than expected in some situations, but feels more PvP-oriented. |

Order is the simplest attribute to handle — Waltz Asherah's follow-ups do most of the work.

## Chaos Attribute Team

With Rosaria's release, **Chaos is now the strongest attribute**.

| Unit | Role | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Rosaria** | Main DPS | Core Chaos anchor. |
| **Lacy** | Secondary DPS | Pairs extremely well with Rosaria. |
| Bunnygirl Charlotte or Lydia | Flexible | Either can be slotted in depending on the matchup. |
| Elisa | Frontline | Sometimes a solid frontline option. |

This is the most powerful comp available right now. Use it as your default whenever Chaos is viable.
